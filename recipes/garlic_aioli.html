Garlic Aioli

Description

Rich and tangy garlic aioli makes the perfect dipping sauce for fries, seafood, sandwiches, and vegetables. With just five ingredients, this incredibly easy dip deserves to be a part of your recipe rotation. Our community of home cooks keeps coming back for more of this creamy condiment that goes with everything â€”try it once and you will too.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Mix mayonnaise, garlic, lemon juice, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving.
